# UserCentricMileStoneProject
## Overview

A website based on a British rock band called Reef. This site is consists of a Home page, a biography of the band - including a brief history of the band and an intro to it's members, a tour page, a media page - which will contain pictures and videos, a store page selling the bands merchandise and finally, a contact page where the site visitors can book the band.

The main purpose of the site is for existing fans but also for new fans who are discovering Reef for the first time. These visitors may wish to find out more about the band and even book them for their own events.

# User Experience (UX)
 - ## User Stories
    - ### What can the first time visitor expect?
        - #### As a first time visitor, I would like to find information on the band and its members
        - #### As a first time visitor, I would like to find a clearly laid out site that is simple to navigate.
        - #### As a first time visitor, I would like to find information on when the band is on tour
        - #### As a first time visitor, I would like to see pictures and videos of live perfomances of the band.
        - #### As a first time visitor, I would like to buy the band's merchandise
        - #### As a first time visitor, I would like to be able to book the band for my own events.
